I believe, dear Jano, that when we say a painting deepens, we must take this image literally. You have dug a lot, and these dramatic impulses that have always driven and constructed your painting are now buried in a more secret humus. You, who seem so fragile, what strength allows you to stir skies and earth until you immerse yourself again in this living original matter that we all seek, through various paths, to rediscover?
Jean Bazaine

My favorite word in the French language? Without hesitation, I answer: fantasy. It is a word of infinite richness, open to all interpretations… all creations… all possibilities… infinite dimensions… and it will never end! Everyone is free to enrich it each day with their heart and spirit, and with the wealth they have received since birth. I think it infinitely surpasses everything that can be said about freedom, hope… It is my preference, what I was born for.

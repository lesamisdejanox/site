Established on July 9, 2024, the association « Friends of Jano Xhenseval » is dedicated to promoting, preserving, and defending the artistic legacy of Jano Xhenseval.

As the custodian of moral rights to the artist's works and owner of some of his creations, the association strives to catalog his achievements, inspire appreciation and admiration for his art, and support events aligned with his artistic vision.

I believe life is only beautiful when it is made light. There is something of the skylark that roams the studio, keeping me from yielding to the temptation of heaviness, discouragement… Something trivial for time, yet important for the soul.

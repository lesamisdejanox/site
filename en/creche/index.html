Jano's Crib

« Jano had created her own crèche, with all its characters, including its angels. One year, while looking at this crèche, a little girl asked her: « I see the shepherds, but where are the shepherdesses? » Jano, amazed by this question, immediately added shepherdesses and pointed them out each year to her visitors, with the memory of this very perceptive little girl ...»

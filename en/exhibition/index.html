Exhibitions

Jano preferred to reserve his exhibitions for the private sphere: this list of exhibitions is not exhaustive.

  • Galerie Larock-Granoff, 13 quai de Conti, Paris 6e: November 17-December 2, 2021.
  • Saint-Antoine des Quinze Vingts Church, Paris 12e (Spring of the Organ, “Astral Path”): April 6-8, 2018.
  • At Anne-Laure de Coincy's residence, Berlin: April 16-18, 2016.
  • At Claudine and Jean-François Bénard's residence, Paris 12e: November 21-23, 2014.
  • Château de Tongre-Notre-Dame (Belgium), “Secrets of the Workshop”: May 17-18, 2014.
  • Jano Xhenseval and Jean-Marie Tézé: “The Glory of the Sensitive,” Centre spirituel de Penboc’h (Morbihan): August 2-14, 2013.
  • Galleries of Hôpital Cognac-Jay, Paris 15e: May 25, 2011.
  • Jano Xhenseval and Jean-Marie Tézé, Mairie du 6e, place Saint-Sulpice: December 2006.
  • Fondation Cognacq-Jay, 15 rue Eugène-Million, Paris 15e: 1996.
  • Crypt of Saint-Lambert de Vaugirard Church, 2 rue Gerbert, Paris 15e: March 26, 1990.
  • Jano Xhenseval and Jean-Marie Tézé, Agora-Sèvres, rue de Sèvres, Paris 6e: May 18-29, 1979.
